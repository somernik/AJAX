jInvertScroll Example - Sarah Omernik

This plugin allows content to move horizontally across the screen with a parallax effect while the user scrolls down. The parallax effect is caused by the different widths of the layers making them look like they are moving at different rates.

Scroll down to see more

This example has 3 layers: the lightblue mountains, the elephants and the divs with paragraphs.

With jInvertScroll you can have as many layers as you want. They must be passed as an array to the jInvertScroll function. For the layers to move at different rates you need to specify different widths and z-indexes in your CSS.
